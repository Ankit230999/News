The commerce sector is in constant flux, with emerging technologies serving both as the driving force and the product of this dynamic. In the digital revolution era, technological innovations are accelerating at an unprecedented pace, impacting a wide range of sectors and professions. Much like the French Revolution that radically transformed society years ago, these technologies could profoundly alter the commercial landscape, creating a new model and new opportunities for those who seize them. However, to take advantage of this evolution, it is essential to understand the trends and methods at play and to anticipate upcoming changes.
